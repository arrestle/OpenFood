Join Open Food - Know where your food dollars go

Why?

  • Choose which farming practices you want to support with your existing food budget
  • Know who benefits when you spend your hard earned dollars
  • Easily identify the impact of your food choices on climate, business owners, and the farm and food processing workers
  • Encourage transparency by supporting OpenFood initiatives in your community
